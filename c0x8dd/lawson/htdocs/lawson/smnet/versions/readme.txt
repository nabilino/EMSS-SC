
Instructions to view version information
----------------------------------------
1) Save versions.tar to your hard drive
2) Two options to extract versions.tar
   a)  FTP versions.tar (in binary) to a Unix Server and untar, then FTP the directory back to your PC
   b)  Open versions.tar with WinZip v12
3) Extract all files directly to your local drive
4) This will create the lawson\smnet\versions directory on your local drive
5) Navigate to lawson\smnet\versions double click index.htm
