Manager Self-Service Forms

Important Note: Supervisor updates for are now included on the updated Employee Change Form. Please discontinue use of the previous Supervisor Change Form beginning immediately.

If you have an entire department changing supervisors due to reorganization, please email your local Human Resources office for instructions on how to submit that information. All other supervisor changes should be submitted on the Employee Change Form.

Form Description
Employee Change Form The Employee Change Form is used to submit changes to an employee's primary position. This form is now a fillable PDF file. Instructions for completion and submission are included on the form. Note: This form replaces the Change of Status Form and Personnel Action Form (Magic Valley).
Employee Change Form - Additional Positions
 The Employee Change Form - Additional Positions is used to submit changes to an employee's additional (non-primary) positions. Use this form to add, remove or modify information for additional positions. This form is now a fillable PDF file. Instructions for completion and submission are included on the form.
Employee Termination Form The Employee Termination Form is used to submit the termination of employment for an employee. This form is now a fillable PDF file. Instructions for completion and submission are included on the form.
