Online W-2 FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

Q: How do I get my Online W-2?
A: Login into myHR, locate the “Online W-2’s” link in the myHR Payroll section.
 
Q: What is my employee ID # for Online W-2?
A:

It is the same as your myHR ID.
 
Q: What is my password for Online W-2?
A: It is the same as your myHR password. If you need to reset your password for myHR please call the myHR password reset line at 381-7713.
 
Q: Why should I receive my W-2 online?
A: Timeliness, access to print at your convenience, access to prior year W-2’s, cost savings for St. Luke’s.
 
Q:

What does authorize or revoke mean regarding my Online W-2?
A:

Authorize – to authorize means to accept and give St. Luke’s the authority to provide your W-2’s online instead of by mail.

Revoke – to revoke means to undo prior permission for St. Luke’s to provide your W-2’s online.  This will trigger your W-2 to be mailed.

All authorizations and revokes must be done prior to the lock out period during printing.
 
Q:

How do I authorize my “Online W-2”?
A:

Refer to the Online W-2s Instuctions available on myHR website in the myHR Payroll section.
 
Q: How do I revoke my “Online W-2"?
A:

Refer to the Online W-2s Instructions available on myHR website in the myHR Payroll section.
 
Q: What if I forget to logout?
A:

You will be automatically logged out after 15 minutes of inactivity.
 
Q:

What if I am unable to view or print my Online W-2?
A:

Contact the HELP DESK at 381-HELP (4357).
