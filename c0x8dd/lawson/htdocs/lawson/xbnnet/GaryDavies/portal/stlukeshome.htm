PTO Balances

Reminder: Effective October 1, 2014 the maximum number of PTO hours you can carry in your bank will be reduced to 240 hours. Please review your PTO balance (click on myHR Payroll) to determine if you need to take time off to draw down your PTO bank before the deadline.

For those employees who elected to use the PTO Sell Back as a way to draw their banks down, the next sell back is September 12, 2014. As a reminder, in order to sell back PTO hours, you must accrue it in the specified timeframes, per IRS regulations. For example, if you elected to sell back 30 hours in September, you must accrue 30 hours between June 1 and August 23, 2014. You will not accrue hours if your balance is at 320.
